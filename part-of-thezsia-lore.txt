Planet orbiting a white dwarf
Name: Thezsia
Description/Lore:
Once upon a time, Thezsia is inhabited by an advanced civilization.

Time passes, and its stars turns into red giant, perishing every single living organism on it.
The star eventually turns into a white dwarf, and Thezsia cools down.

This planet contain valuable resources, making it great place to set up a mining operation. Can you recapture this planet, and replace its original inhabit?
